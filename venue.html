About the venue

Enter the world of majestic and extravagant banqueting with The Westin, Rajarhat, Kolkata, a luxurious 5-star property nestled in the heart of New Town, Kolkata.... This gigantic hotel is home to enchanting banqueting spaces - The Westin Grand Ballroom, Westin Gardens, Capri, and Coral. A perfect place to invite your guests and host a lavish event.

Westin Kolkata Banquet Hall is located in the vicinity of Mother's Wax Museum, which is well-known among the locals. If your outstation guests are arriving at the venue via plane, The Westin, Kolkata is 8.5 km far from the Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport, which takes around 12 minutes to drive down to the destination, via NH12. The guests who are arriving at this place via train, Howrah Junction which is around 19.9 km far from the venue, which is around 40 minutes' drive via NH12. A luxurious suite is offered to guests arriving from a far distance, make your guests comfortable, and enjoy the event
